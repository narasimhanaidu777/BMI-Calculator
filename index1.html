üìö What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measurement that helps assess if you have a healthy body weight for your height. It's calculated by dividing your weight in kilograms by your height in meters squared.

Why it matters

BMI is a screening tool that can indicate potential health risks associated with being underweight, overweight, or obese.
